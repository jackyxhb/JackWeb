1Overview

This policy explains how data is handled in ETPattern for iOS. The product is built for privacy-preserving language learning.

Learning Data Belongs to You

Flashcards, progress records, and study history remain your assets and are never commercialized.

2Data Collection

ETPattern does not collect personal data for analytics or advertising.

❌
No ad tracking
No advertising or cross-app trackers are integrated.
❌
No profile analytics
Study behavior is not sent to analytics dashboards.
❌
No location collection
ETPattern does not request location signals.

3Data Storage & Security

📁
On-device data store
Cards and progress remain in your app container by default.
☁️
CloudKit sync
Optional sync keeps progress consistent across Apple devices.
🧠
On-device learning model
FSRS scheduling runs locally and does not expose learning vectors externally.

4Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • No selling or data brokerage.
  • No sharing with third parties for advertising.
  • Disclosure only when legally required.

5Translation & External Services

🔄
Minimal payload
Only requested words or fragments are sent for translation.
🔐
Encrypted requests
All external service requests are transmitted over HTTPS.

Note: Translation providers operate under their own published privacy policies.

6Privacy Compliance

  • Designed with GDPR alignment in mind.
  • CCPA-compatible data principles.
  • Compliant with App Store privacy disclosure standards.

7Changes to This Policy

Policy text may evolve. Significant changes are communicated clearly through support channels.
