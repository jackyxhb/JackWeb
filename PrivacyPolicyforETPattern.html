4 Data Sharing & Disclosure

Since we do not collect any data, we cannot and do not share, sell, or trade any user information with third parties.

  • We never sell your data to advertisers or data brokers.
  • We never share your data with third-party services without your explicit consent.
  • We may disclose information only if required by law (with prior notice when possible).
  • Your learning data is never used for algorithm training or model improvements.
