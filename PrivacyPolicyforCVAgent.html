1Overview

This policy explains how information is handled in CVAgent for iOS. The app is privacy-first by default.

Your Data is Yours

We do not collect, sell, or broker personal data. CVs, cover letters, and related files stay under your control.

2Data Collection

CVAgent does not collect personal data.

❌
No mandatory account
Core features work without account registration.
❌
No usage analytics
Behavior tracking and event analytics are not embedded.
❌
No ad SDKs
No ad networks or marketing trackers are integrated.

3Data Storage & Security

Your content is stored securely and remains accessible only to you.

📱
On-device container
Data is kept in the app sandbox on your device.
☁️
Private iCloud sync
Optional sync runs in your private Apple storage context.
🔐
Keychain protection
Credentials and keys are protected by iOS Keychain.

4Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell or trade user data.
  • We do not share user data with third parties for marketing.
  • Disclosure happens only when legally required.

5AI Processing & Privacy

🤖
Provider choice
You choose the AI provider used for generation tasks.
🧼
PII scrubbing
Sensitive fields can be removed before external processing.

Note: Provider-side handling follows each provider's published privacy policy.

6Privacy Compliance

  • GDPR principles respected.
  • CCPA expectations addressed.
  • Aligned with App Store privacy requirements.

7Changes to This Policy

Policy updates may occur over time. Material changes are communicated in-app or through support channels.
