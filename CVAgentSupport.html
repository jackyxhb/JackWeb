Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I create my first CV?

Getting started with CVAgent is simple:

  1. Open the app and tap Create New CV.
  2. Enter your personal information, work experience, and education.
  3. Choose a template style that suits your industry.
  4. Let CVAgent's AI optimize and polish your content.
  5. Export your CV as PDF or share directly.

2. How does the AI tailoring feature work?

CVAgent uses advanced AI to customize your CV for specific job applications:

  • Paste a Job Description: Copy the job posting you're applying for.
  • AI Analysis: CVAgent identifies key requirements and keywords.
  • Smart Suggestions: The app recommends which skills and experiences to highlight.
  • One-Tap Apply: Apply suggestions instantly to create a tailored version.

3. My data isn't syncing between devices

CVAgent uses iCloud to keep your data in sync. If you are experiencing issues:

  • Check iCloud: Ensure you are signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices.
  • Enable iCloud Drive: Go to iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Drive and ensure it is enabled.
  • Internet Connection: Syncing requires an active internet connection.

4. How do I export my CV?

CVAgent offers multiple export options:

  • PDF Export: Tap the share icon and select "Export as PDF" for a professional document.
  • Share Directly: Use the share sheet to send your CV via email, AirDrop, or messaging apps.
  • Print: Connect to a printer and print directly from the app.

5. Can I create multiple versions of my CV?

Yes! CVAgent supports creating multiple CV versions:

  • Each tailored version is saved separately.
  • Organize versions by job title, company, or industry.
  • Easily duplicate and modify existing CVs.

Troubleshooting

If the app freezes or crashes:

  1. Force Quit: Swipe up from the bottom of the screen and pause in the middle, then swipe up on the CVAgent app preview to close it.
  2. Restart: Relaunch the app.
  3. Update: Ensure you are running the latest version from the App Store.

Privacy Policy

Your Data is Yours.
CVAgent is designed with privacy first.

  • We do not collect personal information, usage data, or tracking analytics.
  • All your CVs, cover letters, and personal data are stored securely in your private iCloud container.
  • AI processing is performed on-device where possible, and any cloud processing is anonymized.
  • We have no access to your data.
